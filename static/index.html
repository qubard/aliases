Q: What is this?
A CS:GO bind generator which lets you bind multiple say(s) into one bind.

Q: I'm trying to paste everything in the console, but it doesn't work?
The console in CS:GO has a character limit; paste each command line by line.

Q: Why do I lose my binds when I exit the game?
